## Order tracking
You can track your order in two ways:
1. Click the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.
2. Go to northwindgoods.com/track and enter your order number and email.

If your tracking hasn't updated in 48 hours, contact support — we'll investigate right away.

## Returns & refunds
- You can return any unused item within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund.
- Items must be in original condition and packaging.
- To start a return, email support@northwindgoods.com with your order number, or use the Returns portal at northwindgoods.com/returns.
- Return shipping is **free** for US customers (prepaid label). International customers cover return postage.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within **5–7 business days** after we receive the item.
- **Non-returnable:** gift cards, personalized/engraved items, and opened food or grooming products.

## Exchanges
We're happy to exchange for a different size or color. Start an exchange the same way as a return and note the replacement you'd like. If the new item costs more, we'll send a payment link for the difference.

## Payment methods
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay and Google Pay.
We also offer **Klarna** — pay in 4 interest-free installments. All payments are processed securely; we never store your full card number.

## Warranty
- **Electronics & appliances:** 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.
- **All other products:** 90-day quality guarantee.
- Warranty does not cover accidental damage or normal wear. To make a claim, email support with your order number and a photo of the issue.

- **Cast iron:** hand wash, dry immediately, re-oil lightly after each use. Do not put in the dishwasher.
